The Writer

Gray Walker

Founder · Inside The Baseline

I didn't just grow up watching tennis — I played it. As a collegiate player at Point University, I understand the game from the inside: the grind of college tennis, the gap between college and the pro tour, and everything in between.

Inside the Baseline started because I couldn't find a single publication that took both the pro game and college tennis seriously. So I built it. Based in Opelika, Alabama, I cover everything from ATP and WTA tour results to ITA rankings, NCAA tournaments, and the coaches and programs shaping the next generation of pros.
